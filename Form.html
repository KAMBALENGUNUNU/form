Ethereum Builders Challenge DRC | Défi des Bâtisseurs Ethereum en RDC

About the Form

Join the Ethereum Revolution in DRC!

The Ethereum Builders Challenge DRC is a unique opportunity for Congolese students, developers, and entrepreneurs to dive into the blockchain and Ethereum universe. This form is your gateway to participate in an educational and practical program aimed at training a new generation of Web3 developers in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Whether you're a curious beginner or an experienced developer, this form allows you to register for workshops, hackathons, and university communities dedicated to Ethereum. You'll have the opportunity to learn, collaborate, and build blockchain solutions that address local challenges.

About the Challenge: Transforming DRC through Ethereum

The Ethereum Builders Challenge DRC is an educational and practical program aimed at introducing students from all 26 provinces of the DRC to Ethereum development and the blockchain ecosystem.

Challenge Objectives:

  • Education: Provide practical training on Ethereum, smart contracts, and DApps
  • Community: Create Ethereum clubs in 130 universities across the country
  • Innovation: Encourage students to develop blockchain solutions for local problems in finance, identity, and supply chains
  • Representation: Increase participation of Congolese developers in global Ethereum events like DevCon

What You'll Learn:

  • Ethereum and blockchain fundamentals
  • How to develop smart contracts in Solidity
  • How to create decentralized applications (DApps)
  • How to contribute to Ethereum open-source projects

What You'll Build:

  • Functional DApp prototypes
  • Blockchain solutions for local problems
  • A sustainable Web3 community at your university

À Propos du Défi: Transformer la RDC grâce à Ethereum

Le Défi des Bâtisseurs Ethereum en RDC est un programme éducatif et pratique qui vise à introduire les étudiants des 26 provinces de la RDC au développement Ethereum et à l'écosystème blockchain.

Objectifs du Défi:

  • Éducation: Offrir des formations pratiques sur Ethereum, les contrats intelligents et les DApps
  • Communauté: Créer des clubs Ethereum dans 130 universités à travers le pays
  • Innovation: Encourager les étudiants à développer des solutions blockchain pour résoudre des problèmes locaux
  • Représentation: Augmenter la participation des développeurs congolais dans les événements mondiaux Ethereum

Ce que vous allez apprendre:

  • Les bases d'Ethereum et de la blockchain
  • Comment développer des contrats intelligents en Solidity
  • Comment créer des applications décentralisées (DApps)
  • Comment contribuer à des projets open-source Ethereum

Ce que vous allez construire:

  • Des prototypes de DApps fonctionnels
  • Des solutions blockchain pour des problèmes locaux
  • Une communauté Web3 durable dans votre université

Program Timeline | Calendrier du Programme

Phase 1: Introduction and Training (Month 1)

Objective: Discover Ethereum and smart contract basics

Activities: Interactive workshops, online courses, mentoring sessions

Outcome: Understanding of blockchain fundamentals

Phase 2: Community Formation (Months 2-3)

Objective: Create Ethereum clubs in universities

Activities: Practical Solidity training, local hackathons, open-source contributions

Outcome: 130 active Ethereum clubs in universities

Phase 3: DApp Development (Months 4-5)

Objective: Build blockchain applications for local challenges

Activities: Mentoring, project funding, collaboration with Ethereum experts

Outcome: 50+ functional DApp prototypes

Phase 4: Demo Day (Month 6)

Objective: Showcase top projects and celebrate achievements

Activities: Public event with project demos, awards, and funding opportunities

Outcome: Recognition of local talent and sustainable developer network creation
